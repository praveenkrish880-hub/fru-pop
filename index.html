How We Make It 👨‍🍳

Simple, honest, from scratch — every single day

1

Fresh Fruits Only

We source fresh Alphonso mangoes, strawberries, and blueberries daily. No canned syrups, no powders.

2

Blend & Sweeten Naturally

Fruits are blended smooth and sweetened only with dates paste. No refined sugar added.

3

Mix With Pure Cream

We combine fruit puree with fresh Nandini cream and condensed milk for that rich, creamy texture.

4

Pour Into Fruit Moulds

The mixture is poured into beautiful fruit-shaped silicone moulds — mango, strawberry, blueberry shapes!

5

Freeze & Serve Fresh

Frozen overnight at -18°C and served fresh from our kiosk in HSR Layout every evening.
